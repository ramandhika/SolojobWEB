About

SoloJob is an application that connects SMK graduates and owners of small business in the Solo City area with easy and verified jobs/employees.

Problem

Vocational High Schools (SMK) are graduates who have expertise but are the biggest contributor to the unemployment rate in Solo.

Filter and Verifications is an important for jobseeker & company.

Communication distance between jobseeker & company.

No easy ways to find a job that matches with passion.
